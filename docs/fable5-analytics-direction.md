## Codex feasibility adjustments

- `Olist` and `UCI` are candidate data sources, not approved facts. Their exact source ownership, license text, stable acquisition path, and redistribution allowance must be verified before ingestion or publication.
- No Fable-proposed metric may be shown until it is recomputed from a versioned dataset and captured in a verified artifact. The holdout, anomaly, calibration, AUC/KS/Brier, PSI, fairness, and policy figures are implementation requirements, not current claims.
- Credit Policy Lab will begin with a deterministic precomputed policy/backtest slice. ONNX browser scoring is conditional on reproducible model recovery/export; it must not block the policy experience.
- Margin Control Tower will start with one metric family, one diagnosed anomaly, one bounded scenario, and contract checks. Inventory, experiments, and broad lifecycle modules remain stretch scope.
- Both products must label public, inherited, synthetic, and newly computed assets separately and run without the inherited external demos.

## Locked North Stars

1. **Margin Control Tower**: contribution-margin diagnosis and action verification for an e-commerce category manager, backed by governed metric semantics and data-quality contracts.
2. **Credit Policy Lab**: calibrated risk-to-policy decisioning for a credit policy manager, separating score, expected loss, approve/review/decline thresholds, and monitoring.
